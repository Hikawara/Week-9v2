Who are we?

Based in Melbourne, Cari's Architects have an established track record of successful residential and commercial projects in these regions. We have a commitment to quality of design, distinguished by our exceptional skills in empathising with and communicating our clients’ wants and needs. We provide comprehensive client consultation, highly detailed documentation, thorough contract administration, knowledge of the latest in building systems and materials, and a network of reliable consultants and trades people.

We are registered with a number of industry-recognised organisations, including the Australian Institute of Architects and the Housing Industry of Australia. Our open-minded design philosophy treats each new project according to its individual set of circumstances. We pay particular attention to detail throughout the entire architectural process and works tirelessly towards securing a positive outcome. Meet our team

Cari Smith (Founder and CEO)

Cari has more than 25 years’ experience in the architecture and building industry. In fact, she has uniquely built her career from the ground up as an actual carpenter on site, to being a university student, then educator, onto senior design and documenting architect with some of Melbourne’s leading architects and designers. Eventually, founding the awarding winning Cari's Architects with long-term friend, James Henderson, where he is now the Product and design manager of the highly successful Burwood Office.



James Henderson (Product and design manager)

James has some 20 years’ experience in the architecture industry, developing design and documentation skills on literally hundreds of medium- to large-scaled commercial retail projects before being approached by a leading architectural design office where he worked to identify opportunities to improve the delivery of architectural projects. James has been able to successfully deliver many high-quality, bespoke architectural projects through thoughtful planning, detailing and supervision. His ceaseless passion for seeking out the latest in architectural materials, systems and technologies available and sharing his knowledge and insights with clients has proven invaluable on projects from initial sketch design through to detailed design, building documentation and contract administration.
